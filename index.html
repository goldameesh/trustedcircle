Privacy Policy

What we collect

When you use the Bhramaastra Advisory Services DecisionMine Engine, your input (idea, sector, location, and context) is sent to Anthropic's API for processing. We do not store your inputs on our servers beyond what is needed to generate your analysis.

Session data (analyses run, verdicts, sector, mode) is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) for the purpose of displaying your history and powering analytics. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

What we do not do

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Third Brain?

The Third Brain is BAS's proprietary decision intelligence engine — a structured AI-powered system that applies 15 decision layers, 9 scoring axes, and 6 established research frameworks to any idea, decision, or business problem you submit.

How is this different from just asking ChatGPT?

The Third Brain applies a specific, structured methodology — the Hidden Value Conversion Framework — rather than giving a generic response. Every analysis runs the same rigorous architecture. Output is standardised, scored, and traceable to established research. It thinks like a senior advisor, not a search engine.

How long does an analysis take?

Typically 45–120 seconds depending on the mode selected and the complexity of the input. The processing screen shows real-time progress through all 20 analytical steps.

Is my data private?

Your input is processed via Anthropic's API and not stored on our servers. Session history is stored only in your browser. We do not sell or share your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What is the IdeaPlayground™?

The IdeaPlayground™ is designed for people with raw, unformed ideas — before they have a business plan or team. It validates your idea through the full 15-layer framework and presents the most essential findings. You can then unlock the full 20-section analysis and a step-by-step execution path.

Can I regenerate an analysis if the output missed something?

Yes. After every analysis, a Regeneration Box appears at the bottom of your output. Add any corrections, missing context, or new information and the engine regenerates a deeper, more accurate analysis using your original input plus the update.

What are the 5 verdict types?

GO — Proceed with confidence. PILOT — Test before full commitment. CAUTION — Real issues need addressing first. REDESIGN — Core model needs rethinking. STOP — Fundamental blockers exist; do not proceed as structured.

Are the cited sources real?

Yes. The engine is instructed to only cite real, verifiable URLs it can confirm. When uncertain, it will indicate "source to be verified" rather than fabricate a link. Always verify citations independently before relying on them.

How do I get the most out of the platform?

Provide maximum context — your location, background, budget, what you've tried, who your customer is, and what's stopping you. The more specific your input, the more personalised and actionable your output will be.

Is this a replacement for professional advisors?

No. The Third Brain is a decision support tool — not a substitute for qualified legal, financial, or professional advice. Use it to sharpen your thinking, identify blind spots, and prepare better questions for the professionals you engage.
